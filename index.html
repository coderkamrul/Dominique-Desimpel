WHO IS DOMINIQUE DESIMPEL


Our company started in Belgium in 1997 with a very simple premise: discovery. At the very beginning of my career, I fell in love with the beautiful simplicity and natural texture of Moroccan tiles. But the world is big and I was curious to learn more about what was available in other parts of the globe.

I wanted to search for things and discover new, interesting materials that—at that time—were less well known in Belgium and central Europe.

OUR PHILOSOPHY


It's important for me that our products don't reflect a certain fashion, fad or time period. It's important for me that everything we offer is representative of good taste. Whether it is old or new, the things we live with should reflect the way we live now. The materials that we surround ourselves with help us tell the story we want to create in our lives.

OUR ATELIER


Our showroom in Knokke, Belgium,is a library of ideas and our suppliers are artisans in the production and creation of their trade. The designers, architects, and clients that we work with around the world know that the materials we deliver give a spark to their work that illuminates the beauty of life and the creation of remarkable living spaces.
